About lolchance.com

Did your team ever had an awesome comeback in a League of Legends match that felt like a sure defeat mid game?

lolchance.com tells you how close you were to a defeat and visualizes your chance to win during the whole match. The win chance is calculated based on thousands of other matches that had a similar matchup compared by gold, champion levels, dragon, baron and other metrics.
